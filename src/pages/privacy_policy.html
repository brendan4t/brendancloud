Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2025

Thank you for using our Chrome extensions!

We are committed to your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data (or more accurately—how we don’t).

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data or usage data from users of our Chrome extensions. Period.

2. No Tracking, No Analytics

We do not use cookies, trackers, analytics tools, or any other mechanisms to monitor your behavior or gather usage statistics.

3. No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties—because we don’t have any to begin with.

4. Permissions

Some of our Chrome extensions may require limited browser permissions (e.g., to modify certain web pages or access tabs) in order to function properly. These permissions are only used to enable the intended features and not to collect or transmit any user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Since we don’t collect any information, we fully comply with children’s privacy laws such as COPPA.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this policy (though we don’t expect to), we will update the date at the top of this page. We recommend checking back occasionally.
